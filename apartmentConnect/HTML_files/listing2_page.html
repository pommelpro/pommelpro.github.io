2218 Maple Avenue

A beautiful two bedroom apartment overlooking the wonderful terraces of Maple Avenue. Just a few blocks away from campus and close by to eateries Tomate and Rollin' 2 Go. Get groceries at D&D finer food! The complex is home to dozens of friendly students a great staff. Washing machines are closeby and plentiful, as well as a very accessable Engineer! Cable and high speed internet are easily set up and of excellent quality. For the North campus student, this apartment is a must.

Features a spacious galley style kitchen complete with stove, oven, dishwasher, and sink. Hardwood floors line the entire apartment. The bedrooms are spacious and each has it's own heater and ceiling fan. The apartment has 2 bedrooms, but can easily accommodate 3 or 4 tennants, making rent much cheaper. Spacious living room with bay window style openings, great for parties and gatherings. Plenty of light throughout.
